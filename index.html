About Us


The Flying Past celebrates the charm and character of vintage air travel. We share the stories, style, and atmosphere that made flying in earlier decades so memorable. Whether you love classic aircraft, old menus, or retro crew uniforms, we’re here to bring the past back to life.

The History of the Jetliner


The rise of the jetliner transformed air travel from a lengthy journey into a fast, modern experience. From the first commercial jets of the 1950s to the wide-bodied icons of the 1970s, these aircraft reshaped global travel and brought the world closer together. This section explores how the jet age began, the planes that defined it, and the legacy they left in the skies.

Aviation Style Icons


Aviation has produced some of the most recognisable uniforms and looks in modern history. From tailored jackets and bold colours to sleek cabin crew designs of the 1960s and 70s, these styles shaped the image of air travel for generations. This section highlights the people and outfits that turned flying into a true fashion moment.

Cocktails of the Jet Age


Airlines once took great pride in serving elegant drinks high above the clouds, offering passengers cocktails that captured the glamour of early jet travel. From classic martinis to signature inflight mixes, these recipes reflect a time when service felt indulgent and unhurried. This section brings those vintage creations back for you to enjoy at home.
